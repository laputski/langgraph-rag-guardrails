# 401(k) Retirement Plan

Acme Corp offers a 401(k) plan through Fidelity Investments to help employees save for retirement with a generous employer match.

## Employer Match

Acme Corp matches **100% of the first 4% of salary** that an employee contributes to their 401(k). This match vests immediately — it is yours from day one.

**Example**: If your salary is $100,000 and you contribute 4% ($4,000/year), Acme Corp contributes an additional $4,000. If you contribute less than 4%, the match is proportional.

## Contribution Limits (2025)

- Employee contribution limit: **$23,500/year** (IRS limit)
- Catch-up contributions (age 50+): an additional **$7,500/year**
- Roth 401(k) option is available — contributions are after-tax but withdrawals in retirement are tax-free

## Enrollment

- New employees are **automatically enrolled** at a **6% contribution rate** starting on their first day of work.
- You may change your contribution rate or opt out at any time via Fidelity NetBenefits (netbenefits.com/acmecorp).
- Auto-escalation: contribution rate increases by **1% each January** until it reaches 10%, unless you opt out.

## Investment Options

The plan offers a curated menu of low-cost index funds and target-date funds. Acme Corp's default investment is the Fidelity Freedom Index Fund matching your expected retirement year.

## Loans and Hardship Withdrawals

- **Loans**: You may borrow up to 50% of your vested balance (minimum $1,000, maximum $50,000). Must be repaid within 5 years with interest.
- **Hardship withdrawals**: Available for immediate financial need (medical, housing, education). Subject to income tax and 10% early withdrawal penalty if under age 59½.
