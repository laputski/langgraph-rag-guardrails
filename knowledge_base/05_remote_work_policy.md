# Remote Work Policy

Acme Corp supports flexible work arrangements to attract top talent and promote work-life balance.

## Hybrid Work Model (Default)

Acme Corp operates on a **hybrid model**: employees are expected to be in the office at least **3 days per week** (Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday are designated anchor days). Monday and Friday are flexible remote days by default.

Department heads may set stricter requirements based on team needs. Client-facing roles and roles requiring physical presence are exempt and may have different schedules.

## Eligibility for Full Remote Work

Employees may apply for a **full remote arrangement** (0 days in-office) if they meet all of the following criteria:
- Have completed at least **12 months** of employment at Acme Corp
- Have a performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or higher in their last review
- Have manager and HR Business Partner approval
- Have a home workspace that meets the Ergonomics Guidelines (self-certification required)

Full remote approval is reviewed annually and can be revoked if performance declines.

## Equipment for Remote Work

- Acme Corp provides a **company laptop** and necessary peripherals for all employees.
- A one-time **home office stipend of $500** is available for full-remote employees to set up their workspace (receipts required, submit via Concur within 90 days).
- Internet connectivity is the employee's responsibility. Acme Corp does **not** reimburse internet bills.
- All work must be performed on company-provided equipment. Personal devices must not be used for business activities.

## Security Requirements

- Connect to the **Acme Corp VPN** whenever accessing internal systems remotely.
- Do not work from public Wi-Fi without the VPN active.
- Lock your screen whenever you step away, even at home.
- Physical documents containing confidential information must be stored securely and shredded when no longer needed.

## Time Zone Policy

Remote employees must maintain **core hours of 10:00–15:00 in their manager's time zone** for collaboration and meetings. Outside core hours, schedule flexibility is permitted.
