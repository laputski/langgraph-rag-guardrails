# Health Benefits

Acme Corp offers comprehensive medical coverage to all full-time employees and their eligible dependents.

## Medical Plan Options

Acme Corp offers three medical plan tiers through Anthem Blue Cross:

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee Only) | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|---|---|---|---|
| HDHP + HSA | $0 (fully company-paid) | $1,500 | $4,000 |
| PPO Standard | $85 | $500 | $3,000 |
| PPO Premium | $210 | $250 | $2,000 |

For family coverage, add $350/month (HDHP), $450/month (PPO Standard), or $650/month (PPO Premium) to the employee-only premium.

## Health Savings Account (HSA)

Employees enrolled in the HDHP receive a **company HSA contribution of $750/year** (individual) or **$1,500/year** (family), deposited in January. Employees may make additional pre-tax contributions up to IRS limits.

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

All employees (including part-time) have access to Acme Corp's EAP through Lyra Health at **no cost**. The EAP provides:
- Up to **12 free therapy or coaching sessions per year**
- 24/7 crisis line
- Financial and legal counseling
- Work-life services (childcare referrals, elder care support)

Access at: lyrahealth.com/acmecorp or call 1-800-555-LYRA

## Enrollment

- New hires must enroll within **30 days** of their start date in Workday.
- Open enrollment occurs each October for the following plan year.
- Mid-year changes are only permitted after a **Qualifying Life Event** (marriage, birth, loss of other coverage, etc.) — report within 30 days of the event.
