# Sick Leave Policy

Acme Corp provides paid sick leave separately from PTO to ensure employees can care for themselves or family members without financial concern.

## Sick Leave Allowance

All full-time employees receive **10 paid sick days per calendar year**. Part-time employees (20–39 hours/week) receive **5 paid sick days per calendar year**. Sick leave does not accrue — it is granted as a lump sum on January 1st (or on your start date for new hires, prorated by remaining months in the year).

## Eligible Uses of Sick Leave

Sick leave may be used for:
- Your own illness, injury, or medical appointment
- Care for an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, or domestic partner) who is ill
- Mental health days — Acme Corp recognizes mental health as equally important to physical health
- Preventive care appointments (annual physicals, dental cleanings, vision exams)
- Pregnancy-related conditions (used prior to Parental Leave eligibility)

## Reporting Absence

- Notify your manager **before your shift starts** on any sick day. A text or Slack message is sufficient.
- For absences of **3 or more consecutive days**, a doctor's note may be requested at manager discretion.
- Log sick leave in Workday under "Sick Leave" (not PTO).

## Unused Sick Leave

Unused sick leave does **not** carry over to the following year and is not paid out upon termination. The purpose of sick leave is health — not as additional vacation.

## Extended Illness

If you exhaust your sick leave balance during an extended illness, you may:
1. Use accrued PTO to continue paid leave
2. Apply for Short-Term Disability (STD) coverage — see the Benefits & Insurance policy
3. Request unpaid leave under FMLA (if eligible) — see the FMLA policy
