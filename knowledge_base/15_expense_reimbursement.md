# Expense Reimbursement Policy

Acme Corp reimburses employees for reasonable, necessary, and pre-approved business expenses.

## Submission Process

1. Submit all expense reports through **Concur** within **30 days** of incurring the expense.
2. Attach receipts for all expenses over **$25**.
3. Reports are approved by your manager and then processed by Finance.
4. Approved reimbursements are paid via direct deposit within **5 business days** of approval.

## Travel Expenses

### Airfare
- Book at least **14 days** in advance when possible to minimize cost.
- Economy class is required for domestic flights. Business class is permitted for international flights of **8+ hours**.
- Use the Acme Corp travel portal (Concur Travel) to book — out-of-portal bookings may not be reimbursed.

### Hotels
- Maximum rate: **$250/night** (excluding taxes) in standard markets; **$350/night** in high-cost cities (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo). Exceptions require manager and Finance approval.

### Meals (Per Diem)
- Breakfast: up to $20
- Lunch: up to $30
- Dinner: up to $60
- Alcohol is not reimbursable unless explicitly approved for client entertainment.

### Ground Transportation
- Rideshare (Uber/Lyft) and taxis are reimbursable with receipt.
- Personal vehicle mileage is reimbursed at the current IRS standard mileage rate.
- Rental cars require pre-approval from your manager.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are **not reimbursable**:
- Personal entertainment, spa services, or gym fees
- Fines or penalties (parking tickets, speeding fines)
- Airline seat upgrades above economy on domestic flights
- Spouse/partner travel (unless specifically pre-approved as business necessity)
- Expenses without a receipt (over $25)

## Corporate Credit Card

Employees with frequent travel may apply for an Acme Corp corporate credit card through Finance. Cardholders must still submit expense reports in Concur within 30 days of statement close.
