# Equipment Policy

Acme Corp provides the tools necessary for employees to do their best work. This policy governs the provision, use, and return of company equipment.

## Standard Equipment

All full-time employees receive the following at hire:
- **Laptop**: MacBook Pro 14" (M3, 16GB RAM, 512GB SSD) for most roles; Windows equivalents are available on request for roles requiring Windows-specific software.
- **External monitor**: 27" 4K display for office workstations (not provided for home offices unless full-remote approved).
- **Peripherals**: keyboard, mouse, headset, and video conferencing camera for office workstations.

## Requesting Additional Equipment

- Software licenses: submit a request via the IT portal (Jira Service Management) with manager approval.
- Specialized hardware (e.g., GPUs for ML roles, ergonomic equipment): manager approval + HR sign-off required.
- Mobile phone: provided only for roles with on-call responsibilities. Manager must justify need.

## Care and Responsibility

- Employees are responsible for the safekeeping of company equipment.
- Loss or damage due to negligence may result in the employee being charged the depreciated replacement cost.
- Accidental damage should be reported to IT within 24 hours via it-help@acme-internal.com.
- Theft must be reported to IT and your manager immediately, and a police report filed within 48 hours.

## Personal Use

Limited personal use of company laptops is permitted (e.g., checking personal email). However:
- Do not store personal files on company devices.
- Do not install unlicensed software.
- Acme Corp may monitor activity on company devices.

## Equipment Return

- All company equipment must be returned within **5 business days** of your last working day.
- Return equipment to IT via prepaid shipping label (sent by IT upon offboarding initiation) or in person.
- Equipment not returned may result in deduction from final paycheck (where permitted by law).

## Refresh Cycle

Laptops are refreshed every **4 years**. Employees may request an earlier refresh if the device is significantly degraded — subject to IT and manager approval.
