# First Day Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure you complete all required tasks on your first day at Acme Corp.

## Before You Arrive
- [ ] Accept your offer letter and complete pre-hire paperwork (sent via DocuSign)
- [ ] Review the Acme Corp Employee Handbook (emailed with your welcome package)
- [ ] Set up your Acme Corp email account (IT sends credentials 2 business days before start)

## Morning (HR Orientation, 9:00–12:00)
- [ ] Bring government-issued photo ID for I-9 verification
- [ ] Collect your employee badge from Reception (Building A, Lobby)
- [ ] Meet your HR Business Partner for orientation briefing
- [ ] Sign and submit all Day-1 HR forms (emergency contact, direct deposit, tax forms)
- [ ] Enroll in benefits — you have **30 days** from your start date to enroll; elections cannot be changed outside open enrollment except for qualifying life events

## Afternoon (Team & IT Setup, 13:00–17:00)
- [ ] Receive your company laptop from IT — do NOT use personal devices for work
- [ ] Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all company accounts
- [ ] Join required Slack channels: #general, #announcements, #your-team
- [ ] Schedule your first 1-on-1 with your manager within the first week
- [ ] Meet your Onboarding Buddy (introduction email sent by HR)

## End of Day
- [ ] Confirm your direct deposit information in the HR portal (Workday)
- [ ] Log your hours in Workday if you are an hourly employee
- [ ] Review the IT Acceptable Use Policy — acknowledgment required within 3 days
