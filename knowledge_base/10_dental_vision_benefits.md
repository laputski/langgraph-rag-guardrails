# Dental and Vision Benefits

## Dental Coverage

Acme Corp provides dental coverage through **Delta Dental** for all full-time employees and eligible dependents.

### Plan Summary

| Service | Coverage |
|---|---|
| Preventive (cleanings, X-rays) | 100% covered, no deductible |
| Basic restorative (fillings, extractions) | 80% after $50 deductible |
| Major restorative (crowns, bridges, dentures) | 50% after $50 deductible |
| Orthodontia (braces) | 50%, up to $1,500 lifetime max |

Annual maximum benefit: **$2,000 per person**.

### Monthly Premiums

- Employee only: **$8/month**
- Employee + spouse/partner: **$22/month**
- Employee + children: **$28/month**
- Family: **$38/month**

## Vision Coverage

Vision coverage is provided through **VSP** (Vision Service Plan).

### Plan Summary

- **Eye Exam**: $10 copay, once per calendar year
- **Frames**: $150 allowance per year (20% off balance above allowance at VSP network providers)
- **Lenses**: $25 copay for single, bifocal, or trifocal lenses
- **Contact Lenses** (in lieu of frames): $150 allowance per year
- **LASIK discount**: 15% off at LasikPlus network providers

### Monthly Premiums

- Employee only: **$5/month**
- Employee + spouse/partner: **$10/month**
- Employee + children: **$10/month**
- Family: **$15/month**

## Enrollment

Enroll in Workday within 30 days of hire date, or during annual open enrollment in October.
