# Compensation Philosophy

Acme Corp aims to provide competitive, fair, and transparent compensation that attracts and retains top talent.

## Market Positioning

Acme Corp targets the **60th percentile** of the market for base salary, benchmarked against comparable companies in the technology sector using Radford and Mercer survey data. This means we aim to pay above the median but below the top of market, with the remainder of total compensation delivered through equity and benefits.

## Total Compensation Components

1. **Base Salary**: Fixed annual compensation, paid semi-monthly (1st and 15th).
2. **Annual Bonus**: Performance-based, paid in February. Target bonus is 10–20% of base salary depending on level. Payout ranges from 0% to 150% of target based on company and individual performance.
3. **Equity (RSUs)**: Restricted Stock Units vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff (25% after year 1, then monthly vesting for the remaining 75%).
4. **Benefits**: Health, dental, vision, 401k match, and other benefits (see respective policy documents).

## Pay Equity

Acme Corp conducts a **pay equity analysis** annually to identify and remediate unexplained pay gaps across gender, ethnicity, and other protected characteristics. Results are shared with the Board of Directors.

## Salary Bands

Each role is assigned a salary band with a minimum, midpoint, and maximum. Employees are typically hired between the minimum and midpoint. Progression within the band occurs through merit increases. Promotions move an employee to the next band.

Salary bands are reviewed annually and adjusted based on market data. Employees whose current salary exceeds the new band maximum are not subject to a pay cut; their salary is frozen until the band catches up.

## Transparency

Employees may discuss their compensation with colleagues — Acme Corp fully supports this right under the National Labor Relations Act. Managers are not permitted to discourage these discussions.
