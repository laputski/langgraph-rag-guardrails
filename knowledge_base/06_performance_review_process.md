# Performance Review Process

Acme Corp conducts a structured performance review cycle to support employee growth and recognize contributions.

## Review Cadence

- **Annual Review**: Comprehensive review in December covering the full calendar year. Results are tied to compensation adjustments effective February 1st.
- **Mid-Year Check-in**: Informal review in June. Not tied to compensation but used to adjust goals and provide feedback.
- **90-Day Review**: Conducted at the end of the onboarding period for all new hires.

## Rating Scale

| Rating | Description |
|---|---|
| 5 — Exceptional | Consistently exceeds all expectations; role model for peers |
| 4 — Exceeds Expectations | Frequently surpasses goals; high impact contributions |
| 3 — Meets Expectations | Reliably delivers results; solid performer |
| 2 — Needs Improvement | Partially meets expectations; development plan required |
| 1 — Unsatisfactory | Fails to meet expectations; immediate action required |

The expected distribution is approximately: 10% Exceptional, 25% Exceeds, 50% Meets, 13% Needs Improvement, 2% Unsatisfactory.

## Process Steps

1. **Self-Assessment** (employee): Complete by November 15th in Workday. Reflect on goals, achievements, and areas for development.
2. **Peer Feedback** (optional): Managers may request feedback from 2–5 peers nominated by the employee.
3. **Manager Assessment**: Complete by November 30th. Calibrate with HR before finalizing.
4. **Calibration Session**: HR facilitates cross-team calibration sessions in early December to ensure consistency.
5. **Review Conversation**: Manager delivers the final rating and feedback by December 20th.

## Compensation Impact

- Rating 4–5: eligible for merit increase of 4–8% and bonus pool participation
- Rating 3: eligible for merit increase of 1–3%
- Rating 1–2: no merit increase; Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) initiated
