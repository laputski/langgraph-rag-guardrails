# Workplace Safety Policy

Acme Corp is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all employees.

## General Safety Principles

- All employees are responsible for maintaining a safe workplace.
- Report any unsafe conditions to Facilities immediately via facilities@acme-internal.com or Ext. 3001.
- Obey all posted safety signs and instructions.
- Do not block emergency exits, fire extinguisher locations, or first aid stations.

## Emergency Procedures

### Fire or Evacuation
1. Activate the nearest fire alarm pull station.
2. Evacuate via the nearest marked emergency exit — do **not** use elevators.
3. Assemble at the designated muster point (marked on floor maps posted near each exit).
4. Do not re-enter the building until authorized by emergency services.

### Medical Emergency
1. Call 911 immediately for life-threatening emergencies.
2. Contact the front desk (Ext. 0) for non-emergency medical assistance.
3. AED defibrillators are located at each floor's main reception area.
4. First aid kits are located in each kitchen/break room.

## Incident Reporting

Any workplace injury, illness, or near-miss must be reported to HR and Facilities within **24 hours** using the Incident Report form in Workday. This applies even if no medical treatment is required. OSHA-required records are maintained by HR.

## Ergonomics

- Each employee is entitled to an **ergonomic assessment** at their workstation upon request.
- Request via Facilities (facilities@acme-internal.com).
- Full-remote employees may request a virtual ergonomic assessment.
- Ergonomic equipment (monitor stands, keyboard trays, ergonomic chairs) may be provided with Facilities approval.

## Alcohol and Drugs

Acme Corp maintains a **drug-free workplace**. Employees are prohibited from:
- Being under the influence of alcohol or controlled substances during work hours
- Consuming alcohol on company premises (except at Acme-hosted events where alcohol is explicitly permitted)
- Using, possessing, or distributing controlled substances on company premises

## Violence Prevention

Acme Corp has a zero-tolerance policy for workplace violence, including threats, intimidation, or physical altercations. Report any concerns to HR or Security immediately. The anonymous Ethics Hotline (1-800-555-ETHICS) is also available.
