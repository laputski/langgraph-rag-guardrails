# Employee Referral Program

Acme Corp's best hires often come from employee referrals. We reward employees who help us find great talent.

## Referral Bonus

| Role Level | Referral Bonus |
|---|---|
| Individual Contributor | $2,000 |
| Manager / Senior IC | $3,500 |
| Director and above | $5,000 |

The bonus is paid in **two installments**:
- 50% after the referred hire completes **30 days** of employment
- 50% after the referred hire completes **6 months** of employment

Bonuses are subject to standard income tax withholding.

## Eligibility Rules

**Who can refer**: Any active, full-time Acme Corp employee (not contractors or part-time employees under 20 hours/week).

**Who can be referred**: Any external candidate who is not a current Acme Corp employee or a former employee who left within the last 6 months.

**Exclusions**:
- Candidates already in the recruiting pipeline (submitted within the last 12 months)
- Candidates being recruited by an external agency for the same role
- Referrals from employees who directly manage the hiring team (to avoid perceived bias)

## How to Submit a Referral

1. Go to Workday → **Recruiting → Submit a Referral**
2. Enter the candidate's name, email, and phone number
3. Attach their resume (optional but recommended)
4. Select the job opening you are referring them for (or "General Interest" if no specific role)
5. The recruiting team will reach out to the candidate within **5 business days**

## Referral Status

You can track the status of your referral in Workday under "My Referrals." You will receive email notifications at key stages (interview scheduled, offer extended, hired).
