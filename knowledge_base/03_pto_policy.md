# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

Acme Corp provides Paid Time Off (PTO) to support employee well-being and work-life balance.

## PTO Accrual — Full-Time Employees

| Years of Service | PTO Days per Year |
|---|---|
| 0–2 years | 15 days (1.25 days/month) |
| 3–5 years | 20 days (1.67 days/month) |
| 6–10 years | 25 days (2.08 days/month) |
| 10+ years | 30 days (2.5 days/month) |

PTO accrues monthly from the first day of employment. You may begin using accrued PTO after **90 days** of continuous employment (the onboarding period).

## PTO Accrual — Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees (20–39 hours/week) accrue PTO at 50% of the full-time rate. Employees working fewer than 20 hours per week are not eligible for PTO.

## PTO Carryover

Employees may carry over up to **5 unused PTO days** into the following calendar year. Any unused days beyond 5 are forfeited on January 1st. Acme Corp does **not** pay out unused PTO at year-end (except upon termination, where applicable by state law).

## PTO Request Process

1. Submit PTO requests in **Workday** at least **5 business days** in advance for requests of 1–5 days.
2. For requests of 6 or more consecutive days, submit at least **3 weeks** in advance.
3. Manager approval is required. Requests may be denied if business needs require coverage.
4. Emergency or unplanned absences: notify your manager as early as possible on the day of absence.

## Company Holidays

Acme Corp observes **11 paid federal holidays** per year. Holidays do not count against your PTO balance. The holiday schedule is published on the company intranet each November for the following year.
