# Onboarding Overview

Welcome to Acme Corp! This document provides an overview of the onboarding process for all new employees.

## Duration

The standard onboarding period at Acme Corp is **90 days**. During this time, new hires are expected to complete mandatory training, meet their team, and ramp up to full productivity.

## Onboarding Phases

### Week 1 — Orientation
- HR paperwork and benefits enrollment (must be completed within 5 business days)
- IT setup: laptop provisioning, accounts, VPN access
- Introduction to Acme Corp values and culture
- Meet your manager and direct team

### Weeks 2–4 — Foundation
- Role-specific training modules via the Learning Portal
- Shadowing sessions with experienced colleagues
- First 1-on-1 with your manager to set 30-day goals

### Days 31–90 — Ramp-up
- Begin contributing to team deliverables
- 60-day check-in with HR and manager
- Complete all mandatory compliance trainings (Code of Conduct, Anti-Harassment, Data Security)
- 90-day performance conversation with manager

## Onboarding Buddy

Every new employee is assigned an **Onboarding Buddy** — a tenured Acme Corp employee outside your direct team who can answer informal questions and help you navigate company culture. Buddy assignments are made by HR on your first day.

## Key Contacts

- **HR Business Partner**: your first point of contact for any HR-related questions
- **IT Helpdesk**: it-help@acme-internal.com or Slack #it-help
- **Facilities**: facilities@acme-internal.com for badge, parking, and office access issues
