# Parental Leave Policy

Acme Corp is committed to supporting employees as they grow their families. We offer generous paid parental leave for all new parents.

## Leave Entitlement

| Parent Role | Paid Leave Duration |
|---|---|
| Primary caregiver (birth, adoption, or foster) | **16 weeks** fully paid |
| Secondary caregiver | **6 weeks** fully paid |

"Primary caregiver" is self-designated. Both parents in a two-parent household may designate themselves as primary if both are Acme Corp employees, but the total paid leave per birth/adoption event is capped at 22 weeks combined.

## Eligibility

- Must have completed **6 months** of continuous employment at Acme Corp before the expected birth/placement date.
- Available for birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child under 18.
- Part-time employees (20+ hours/week) are eligible for proportional paid parental leave.

## Pay During Leave

During parental leave, employees receive **100% of their base salary**. Variable pay (bonus, commissions) is not included. RSU vesting continues during leave.

## How to Request

1. Notify your manager and HR at least **8 weeks** before the expected start of leave (where possible).
2. Submit a leave request in Workday under "Leave of Absence → Parental Leave."
3. HR will confirm your leave dates, pay continuation, and benefits status within 5 business days.

## Leave Timing

- Leave must begin within **12 months** of the birth or placement of the child.
- Leave may be taken continuously or intermittently (minimum 1-week blocks) with manager agreement.
- Leave runs concurrently with any FMLA entitlement.

## Return to Work

Employees returning from parental leave are guaranteed the same or an equivalent position. Managers should schedule a re-onboarding meeting in the employee's first week back.
