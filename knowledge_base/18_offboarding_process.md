# Offboarding Process

When an employee leaves Acme Corp — voluntarily or involuntarily — we follow a structured offboarding process to ensure a smooth transition.

## Notice Period

- **Individual contributors**: 2 weeks standard notice is requested (not legally required for at-will employees).
- **Managers and above**: 4 weeks notice is requested.
- Acme Corp reserves the right to place employees on **garden leave** during their notice period (paid but not required to work).

## Resignation Process

1. Submit written resignation to your manager and HR (email is acceptable).
2. HR will send an offboarding checklist within 1 business day.
3. Your last day is confirmed in Workday.

## Knowledge Transfer

During the notice period, you are expected to:
- Document your key processes, ongoing projects, and contacts
- Transition responsibilities to your manager or a designated colleague
- Complete a handover document (template available in Confluence)

## Exit Interview

HR will schedule a 30-minute exit interview to understand your experience and gather feedback. Participation is voluntary but appreciated. Responses inform our retention and culture efforts.

## Final Pay

- Final paycheck includes base salary through your last day, plus payout of accrued but unused PTO (where required by state law — California, Colorado, etc.).
- Final pay is delivered via your usual direct deposit on the next regular pay date, or sooner if required by state law.

## Benefits Continuation

- Health, dental, and vision coverage continues through the **last day of the month** in which employment ends.
- COBRA election information is mailed within 14 days of coverage termination. You have **60 days** to elect COBRA.
- 401(k) account remains with Fidelity; you may roll it over or leave it.

## Equipment Return

All company equipment must be returned within **5 business days** of your last day. IT will send a prepaid shipping label or schedule an in-person return.

## System Access

All system access is revoked at 5:00 PM on your last working day. Your company email will be forwarded to your manager for 30 days.
